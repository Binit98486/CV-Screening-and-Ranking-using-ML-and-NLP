    About Us

    Welcome To Cv Ranking and Screening

    Here we introduce a platform for resume categorization. You can either upload your resume as a single one or as a set of resumes. This platform will absolutely help you to avoid your burden to select a person for a particular position. If you are belonging to a multinational company, you may be tired of selecting the candidate for a specific position. Here we rectify your problem. You can easily upload the resume set and you can download the zip file which contains different folders of different categories in which there are resumes belongs to the particular categories. Here there are about 25 different categories which include accountant, advocate, agricultural, apparel, architects, arts, automobile, aviation, BPO, banking, building & constructions, business development, consultant, designing, digital media, education, engineering, finance, food & beverage, HR, health & fitness, information technology, management, public relation, sales.
